Yeast are a very simple eukaryote and are very easy to maintain, coupled with a low doubling time, are very useful in research. Many yeast processes are also conserved in humans.
Size: 5-10 $\mu$m
Doubling Time: 2 hours
Genome: 12M base pairs and 6000 genes
##### Applications
- Cell cycle and division
- Protein folding and quality control
- Genetic pathways
