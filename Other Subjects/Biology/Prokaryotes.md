Prokaryotes are single celled organisms that are usually represented by bacteria, blue green algae, mycoplasma, PPLO (Pleuro Pneumonia Like Organisms).
They come in the following shapes:
- Bacillus - Rod like
- Spirillum - Spirals
- Vibrio - Comma shaped
- Cocci - Spherical
