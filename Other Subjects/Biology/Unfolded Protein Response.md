In the case where proteins are misfolded in ER, the following response occurs:
1. Misfolded proteins signals need for more chaperone proteins by activation of trans-membrane kinase.
2. Activated kinase turns into endoribonuclease.
3. Endoribonuclease cuts specific RNA molecules at two positions, removing an intron
4. Two exons are ligated to form mRNA
5. mRNA is translated to make a gene regulatory protein
6. Gene regulatory protein enters nucleus and activates genes encoding ER chaperones
7. Chaperones are made in ER, where they help in folding proteins
