The energy for secondary active transport is derived from energy that has been stored in the form of ionic concentration differences between two sides of a membrane.
It is of two types:
#### Symport
Transports molecules/ions in the same direction at once across the membrane.
#### Antiport
Able to cotransport two different molecules/ions at the same time in two directions across the membrane.
