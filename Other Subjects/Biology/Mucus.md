#### Components
- *Water* - 90-95% : Fluidity
- *Mucins (Glycoproteins)*: Gives mucus its viscous, gel like properties
- *Proteins and Enzymes*: Antimicrobials like lysozyme
- *Salts and Lipids*: Electrolytes and lipids keep it structurally integrated'
- *Cells*: Dead WBCs and epithelial cells
#### Functions
- **Protection**: Barrier against bacteria, viruses and dust
- **Lubrication**: Moistens tissue in nose, mouth, throat and gut
- **Immunity**: Contains antibodies (IgA) and enzymes
