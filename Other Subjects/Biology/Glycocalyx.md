Outermost layer of bacterial cell. Protects the cell and helps in adhesion too. 
It is of two forms:
##### Slime Layer
Loose gelatinous sheath, mostly made of polysaccharides
##### Capsule
Thick and tough, made of polysaccharides and proteins

The functions of the glycocalyx are:
- Prevents desiccation (drying)
- Host enzyme protection
- Immune evasion
- Virulence
- Antibiotic resistance
