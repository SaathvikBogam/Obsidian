The ER is the cell organelle that plays an important role in lipid and protein biosynthesis for most of the cell's organelles, including itself.
The ER's membrane separates its lumen from the cytosol. It is organised into net-like branching tubules throughout the cytosol.
There are two types of ER: Rough and Smooth, which contains and does not contain ribosomes respectively.
