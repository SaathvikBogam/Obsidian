Cell membrane is composed mainly of lipids and proteins.  The major lipids are phospholipids arranged in a bilayer. Lipids are arranged in membrane in such a way that polar head is towards the outer side and hydrophobic end is inside.
This ensures non-polar tail is protected from aqueous environment.
