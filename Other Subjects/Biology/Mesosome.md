Special membrane structure formed by extensions of plasma membrane, in the form of vesicles, tubules and lamellae. They are the result of invagination (inward folding) of plasma membrane.
They are of two types:
##### Septal
Connected with nucleoid and assists in replication and cell division.
##### Lateral
Contains respiratory enzymes and performs similar functions to eukaryotic mitochondria, hence also called **Chondroid**.
