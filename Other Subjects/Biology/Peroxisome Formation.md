The peroxisome membrane contains import receptor proteins. The copy of these proteins are synthesized by cytosolic ribosomes and then imported into the organelle. In the process, the lipids required to make the membrane are also imported. From this, the peroxisome fissions into two daughter peroxisomes. This implies that the peroxisomes are though they have formed only from a pre-existing peroxisome.
