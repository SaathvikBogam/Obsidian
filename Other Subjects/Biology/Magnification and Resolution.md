*Magnification*: The ability to enlarge an image of an object
$$
\text{Total magnification}=\text{Magnification of ocular}\times\text{Magnification of objective}
$$
**Resolution**: Ability to distinguish between two closely positioned objects. Smaller values of $D$ imply a better resolution.
$$
D= \frac{0.61\lambda}{n\sin\alpha}
$$
where $\alpha$ is the angle aperture/half angle of cone of light, $n$ is the refractive index and $\lambda$ is the wavelength of light.
