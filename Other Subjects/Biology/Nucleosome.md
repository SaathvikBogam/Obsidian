Nucleosome is the basic unit that composes chromosome/chromatin. It contains histone octamer (two copies of H2A, H2B, H3 and H4), which is wrapped by 146-150 bp of DNA.
MNase (Micrococcal Nuclease) can preferentially break DNA in multiples of 200bp between nucleosomes, which is one of the evidence to support nucleosome.
