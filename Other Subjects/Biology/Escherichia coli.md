E. coli is a prokaryote used to study biochemistry and molecular biology. Its simplicity and ease of propogation make it useful. The small genome size allows for genetic manipulation and analysis.
Size: 1-2 nm
Doubling Time: 20 mins
Genome: 4.6M base pairs and >4000 genes
##### Applications
- Gene expression and regulation
- Protein production
- DNA replication and repair
