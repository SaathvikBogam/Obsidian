Large complex of RNA ,rRNA and proteins. Associated with plasma membrane in prokaryotes and endoplasmic reticulum in eukaryotes. They are not membrane bound.
They are the site of protein synthesis.
Made of two subunits: 
- 50S and 30S which combine to form a 70S prokaryotic ribosome
- 60S and 40S which combine to form a 80S eukaryotic ribosome
