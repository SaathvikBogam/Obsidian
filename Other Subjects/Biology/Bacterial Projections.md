#### Flagella
Helps in motility of the bacterial cell.
Composed of three parts:
- Filament (Globular flagellin proteins)
- Hook (Curved proteinaceous structure)
- Basal Body (Ring like proteins)
#### Pili
Surface structures that do not help in motility.
Pili are hair like, elongated tubular shaped structures made of protein called pilin.
Responsible for formation of conjugation tube.
#### Fimbriae
Fimbriae are small bristle like fibres sprouting of the cell.
They have no role in motility, but they help in attachment of bacteria to host tissue.
