Chromatin is the long string structure made up of nucleosomes and DNA that forms the chromosome. It protects DNA, prevents tangling, and regulates gene expression, DNA replication, and repair.
It is of two types:
##### Euchromatin
Less condensed, transcriptionally active and accessible.
##### Heterochromatin
Highly condensed, transcriptionally inactive and structurally rigid. Often found at centromeres and telomeres.
