Chromosomes are thread like structures located in eukaryotic nuclei. Each chromosome is made of a single DNA molecule and protein. It is the package of DNA containing all the genetic material required for reproduction of that cell.
In most chromosomes, the very long thin DNA fibres are coated with nucleosome forming packaging proteins called *histones*. Aided by chaperone proteins, histones bind to and condense the DNA to maintain its integrity.
