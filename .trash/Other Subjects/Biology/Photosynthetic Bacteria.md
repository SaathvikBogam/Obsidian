In these bacteria, a specialized, membrane associated vesicle called *chromatophore* that functions the site of photosynthesis, housing pigments like chlorophyll and carotenoids.
Gas vacuoles are found in blue green, purple and green bacteria to vary their buoyancy and enable them to float at desired depth by inflating and deflating vesicles.
