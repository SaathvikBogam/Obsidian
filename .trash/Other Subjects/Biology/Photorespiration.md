Although RuBP preferentially adds $CO_{2}$ to Ribulose 5-Phosphate, it can use $O_{2}$ as a substrate in place of $CO_{2}$ when its concentration is low. This is called photorespiration, whose ultimate effect is to use up $O_{2}$ and liberate $CO_{2}$ without the production of useful energy stores. About $\frac{1}{3}^{rd}$ of $CO_{2}$ in plants is lost to photorespiration as $CO_{2}$.
