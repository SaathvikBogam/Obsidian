The nucleus is the site of DNA storage in the cell, in the form of chromosomes.
Typical cells usually have only one nucleus, but some cells like liver cells/hepatocytes can have multiple nuclei because of a property called polyploidy.
Multinucleation is thought to better liver's detoxification and metabolic practices.
