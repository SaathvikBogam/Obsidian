For an $m\times n$ matrix $A$ with column vectors $a_{1},a_{2},\dots a_{n}$, the column space of $A$ is defined as the set of every linear combination of its column vectors
$$
col(A)=\{u\,|\,u=Av,\text{where}\,\,v\in \mathbf{C}^n\}
$$
$$or$$
$$
col(A) = \{x_{1}a_{1}+x_{2}a_{2}+\dots x_{n}a_{n}\,\,|\,\,x_{1},x_{2},\dots x_{n}\in \mathbf{C}\}
$$
