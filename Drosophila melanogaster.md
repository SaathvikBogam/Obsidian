It is easy to maintain and breeds rapidly, with a short cycle of 2 weeks. Over 60% of humans genes have counterparts in Drosophila. Studies using it have improved understanding of molecular mechanisms in animal development, especially regarding body plan formation.
Size: 3 mm
Lifespan: 2-3 weeks
Genome: 180M base pairs and 14000 genes
##### Applications
- Cell signaling
- Neurobiology
- Gene expression
- Cell development
