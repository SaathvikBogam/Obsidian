#### Compound/Bright Field Microscope
![[Pasted image 20260420222359.png|450]]

The main components are:
1. Light Source: Illuminates the specimen
2. Condenser Lens: Collects diffused rays and illuminates specimen with a small cone of light
3. Objective Lens: Collects light rays focused on the specimen by condenser lens
4. Ocular/Projection Lens: Image formed by objective is used an object by a second lens system to form an enlarged image.
5. Specimen: Stained/Unstained; Whole cells/tissue sections
