Common name: Mouse
Size: 6-11 cm in length
Lifespan: 10-18 months
Genome: 2.7B base pairs and 22000 genes
##### Advantages
- Biological similarity to humans, sharinf many diseases for same genetic reason due 95% genetic similarity
- Easy to genetically manipulate to mimic human condition
- Inbred strains provide genetic uniformity
- Short lifespan lets researches study an entire life cycle in 1-2 years
- Cost effective, well understood and easy to transport
##### Types of Mice models
1. Wild Type: Normal, unaltered
2. Knock-out: Genetically engineered with specific genes knocked out
3. Transgenic: Foreign mice are inserted
4. Conditional Knock-out: Genes are deleted in specific tissues or times
5. Knock-in: Gene is replaced or inserted at a specific location
6. Humanized: Mice expressing human genes, tissues or cells
##### Applications
- Gene function studies
- Human disease modeling
- Drug testing
- Immune system research
