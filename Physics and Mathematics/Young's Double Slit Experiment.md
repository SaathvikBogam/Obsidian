In 1801, Thomas Young demonstrated his famous Double Slit experiment to show the wave nature of light.
#### Setup
Two coherent sources of  monochromatic light or a singular monochromatic light source is placed at an appropriate distance away from two slits which are separated by a distance $d$ The slits are at a distance $D$ away from the screen.
![[Pasted image 20260218231309.png]]

#### Observations
Young noted that the light interference produced a alternating bands of bright and dark fringes on the screen.
