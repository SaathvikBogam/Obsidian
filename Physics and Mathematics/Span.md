Given vectors $v_{1},v_{2,\dots}v_{n}\in V$,the span of these vectors is given by:
$$
span(v_{1},v_{2},\dots v_{n})=\{\alpha_{1} v_{1}+\alpha_{2} v_{2}+\dots\alpha_{n}v_{n}\,\,|\,\,\alpha_{1},\alpha_{2},\dots \alpha_{n}\in \mathbf{C}\}
$$
It is the set of all linear combinations of the given vectors, and the span is always a subspace of $V$.
