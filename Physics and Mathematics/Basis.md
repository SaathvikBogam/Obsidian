A basis $\mathbf{B}$ of a vector space $V$ is the set of linearly independent vectors such that the span of those vectors is $V$.
$$
\mathbf{B}=\{v_{1},v_{2},\dots v_{n}\}\,\,\text{where}\,\,v_{1},v_{2},\dots v_{n} \,\,\text{are linearly independent}
$$
$$
span(\mathbf{B})=V
$$
