A vector space is a set $V$ along with addition on $V$ and a scalar multiplication on $V$, the following properties hold true: 
##### Commutativity
$u+v=v+u\quad\forall \,u,v\in V$
##### Associativity
$(u+v)+w=u+(v+w)\quad \forall \,u,v\in V$
##### Additive Identity
$\exists\,\,\text{an element}\,\,0\in V\ni v+0=v\quad\forall \,v\in V$
##### Additive Inverse
$\forall\,v\in V\,\exists\,-v\in V\ni v+(-v)=0$
##### Multiplicative Identity
$1v=v\quad\forall\, v\in V$
##### Distributive Properties
$a(u+v)=au+av\,\,\text{and}\,\,(a+b)v=av+bv\quad\forall\, a,b\in \mathbf{C}\,\,\text{and}\,\,\forall\,u,v\in V$
