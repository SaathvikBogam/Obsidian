The Schrödinger Equation is essentially an equation of motion, just like Newton's 2nd Law $(F=m\ddot{x})$, which we solve to obtain the wave function of a particle. It is a postulate just like Newton's 2nd Law, but it can be motivated using a series of assumptions.
$$
i\hbar \frac{\partial\Psi}{\partial t}=-\frac{\hbar^2}{2m}\nabla \Psi + V\Psi 
$$
