Given below are the properties of Hermite Polynomials.
$$
H_{n}(\xi)=\sum_{j=0}^{\infty}a_{j}\xi^{j}
$$
$$
1.\ \text{For odd }n,\ a_{0}=0\ \text{and for even }n,\ a_{1}=0 
$$
$$
2.\ a_{j+2}=\frac{2(j-n)}{(j+1)(j+2)}a_{j}
$$
$$
3.\ H_{n}(\xi)=(-1)^{n}e^{ \xi^{2} }\left( \frac{ d  }{ d x }  \right)^{n}e^{ -\xi^{2} }
$$
$$
4.\ H_{n+1}(\xi)=2\xi H_{n}(\xi)-2nH_{n-1}(\xi)
$$
$$
5.\ \frac{ d H_{n} }{ d \xi } =2nH_{n-1}(\xi)
$$
$$
6.\ H_{n}(\xi)=\left( \frac{ d  }{ d z }  \right)^{n}e^{ -z^{2}+2z\xi }\ \bigg|_{z=0}
$$
