Western blotting/immunoblotting is a powerful, widely used technique that can detect or semiquantify a specific protein from complex mixtures of proteins. The fundamental principle includes equal protein loading, MW based seperation, electrophoretic transfer to a suitable membrane and antibody based detection. The applications of Western blotting are:
- Detecting a specific protein in a simple or complex protein mixture and semi quantification to determine the relative amounts of protein.
- Protein purification and
