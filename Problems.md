A function $f:[a,b]\rightarrow \mathbb{R}$ is said to be Riemann integrable on $[a,b]$ if for a tagged partition $\dot{\mathcal{P}}$ on $[a,b]$ $\exists\ L\in \mathbb{R}\ni\forall\ \epsilon>0\ \exists\ \delta>0\ni \lvert\lvert \dot{\mathcal{P}} \rvert\rvert<\delta$ then
$$
|S(f;\dot{\mathcal{P}})-L|<\epsilon
$$
