Position Space $\rightarrow$ Momentum Space
$$
\Phi(p,t)=\frac{1}{\sqrt{ 2\pi \hbar }}\int_{-\infty}^{+\infty} e^{ -\frac{ip}{\hbar} x}\Psi(x,t) \, dx 
$$
Momentum Space $\rightarrow$ Position Space
$$
\Psi(x,t)=\frac{1}{\sqrt{ 2\pi \hbar }}\int_{-\infty}^{+\infty} e^{ \frac{ip}{\hbar}x }\Phi(p,t) \, dp
$$
